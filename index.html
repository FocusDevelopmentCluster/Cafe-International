About Us

Cafe International was created to bring the worlds finest cuisine all together in one place. Founded in 2015 Cafe International is proud to feature flavors from all across the world and strives to present them as authentic as possible.

Cuisine Trivia

How many styles of food cuisine are there?

Chinese, Italian, Thai, Indian, French, Japanese, and Mexican cuisines are widely recognized as the top seven global styles of cuisine. These culinary traditions have been passed down for many generations.
